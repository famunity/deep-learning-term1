## Vector Operatiions
### Addition
V + W = [V1+W1, V2+W2, ...]

### Subtraction
V - W = [V1-W1, V2-W2, ...]

### Scalar Multiplication
V * n = [V1*n, V2*n, ...]

### Magnitude and Direction
magnitude of v = distance between P(start) and Q(end)
勾股定理 ||v||^2 = Vx^2 + Vy^2
||v||^2 = V1^2 + V2^2 + V3^2 + ... + Vn^2

#### Normalization: 
process of finding a unit vector in the same direction as a given vector

u = 1/||v|| * v

||u|| = 1 always.
